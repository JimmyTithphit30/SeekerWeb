Our Story

Born from the legacy of SEASAW, SEAKERS is the natural evolution serving graduates, working professionals, and emerging cultural leaders across Europe.

The Challenge We Address

Southeast Asians make up a vibrant and growing population in Europe, yet many face a lack of representation, cross-cultural invisibility, and fragmented community support after university.

SEA professionals often feel torn between assimilation and invisibility. SEAKERS changes that by providing an intentional space centered on shared identity, cultural pride, and professional growth.

Our Vision

To become Europe's leading Southeast Asian professional and cultural network, rooted in community, creativity, and care.
